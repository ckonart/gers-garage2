Do you know what is Detailing?

Full wash

Detailing starts with a deep and particular wash to the type of dirt and condition of your vehicle's paint. Special shampoos and even a degreaser are utilized to remove more difficult dirt such as insects and tar. To complete the process, the vehicle is dried with a microfiber towel to not damage the paintwork..

Ceramic coating

Ceramic coatings bond with your vehicleâ€™s surface to create an extremely glossy, hydrophobic and hard layer which will protect the paintwork. Vehicle receives a multi-stage machine polish, eradicating 90-98% of swirls and holograms.

Wheel Cleaning

The wheels that were seen only as an essential part of the vehicle today have become an instrument of aesthetic evaluation, saying a lot about the owner's care for the vehicle and that's why Detailing brings special care to them! More than a simple wash, we restore and use high quality specific products to make them shine brighter than when they were new.

Interior Detailing

Specific to the interior of the vehicle, it reinforces the interior care of the car from the floor to the roof. It is worth mentioning that cleaning is not just a visual issue, as this procedure also takes care of the hygiene of all parts and pieces inside the vehicle. Thus, the service removes germs and bacteria caused by the accumulation of dirt.
